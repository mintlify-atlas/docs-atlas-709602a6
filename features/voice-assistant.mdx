## Overview

Luna is Paw & Care's AI-powered voice assistant that answers phone calls, triages pet emergencies, and schedules appointments—all through natural voice conversations. Built on Retell AI technology, Luna provides 24/7 phone coverage without requiring staff availability.

<Info>
  **Real Voice, Real Conversations**
  
  Luna uses advanced conversational AI to have natural phone conversations with pet owners. She listens, understands context, and responds by voice in real-time.
</Info>

## Starting a Call

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure Agent (Optional)">
    Click **Settings** to enter a custom Retell Agent ID, or use the default agent from your environment configuration (VITE_RETELL_AGENT_ID).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Start Call">
    Press the green **Start Call** button. Your browser will request microphone access—click Allow.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for Connection">
    Luna connects in 2-3 seconds. You'll see "Connecting to Luna..." then "Live Call" when ready.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Speak Naturally">
    Talk to Luna as you would to a real receptionist. She understands natural language and context.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  **Microphone Access Required**
  
  Your browser must have microphone permissions to connect voice calls. Make sure to allow access when prompted.
</Warning>

## Call Interface

### Status Display

The call interface shows real-time connection status:

- **Ready** (gray): Idle, ready to start a call
- **Connecting** (yellow): Establishing connection to Luna
- **Live Call** (green): Active conversation with Luna
- **Call Ended** (gray): Completed call, ready to start new call
- **Error** (red): Connection failed or technical issue

### Live Controls

During an active call:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Mute/Unmute">
    Click the microphone icon to mute your audio. Luna won't hear you, but she can still speak. Muted calls show a red microphone icon.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="End Call">
    Click the red **End Call** button to disconnect. The call is automatically saved to your database.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Duration Timer">
    Live call duration displays in MM:SS format (e.g., "02:35" for 2 minutes, 35 seconds).
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Live Transcription

As the call progresses, you see a real-time transcript:

- **Luna's messages** appear on the left with a stethoscope icon (🩺)
- **Your messages** appear on the right with a speech icon (🗣️)
- **Speaking indicator**: Three animated dots show when Luna is talking
- **Auto-scroll**: Transcript automatically scrolls to the latest message

<Tip>
  The transcript is saved automatically when the call ends—no manual action needed.
</Tip>

## Live Triage Panel

The right sidebar displays AI-powered triage analysis in real-time:

### Urgency Score

A circular gauge shows the urgency score (0-10) with color coding:

- **Red border**: Emergency (score 9-10)
- **Orange border**: Urgent (score 5-8)
- **Green border**: Routine (score 3-4)
- **Blue border**: Informational (score 0-2)

### Triage Levels

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Emergency">
    **Red Badge** - Immediate veterinary attention required
    
    Detected keywords: bleeding, seizure, unconscious, not breathing, poisoned, hit by car, choking, collapse
    
    **Action**: Recommend immediate emergency visit or transfer to emergency clinic
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Urgent">
    **Orange Badge** - Same-day or next-day appointment needed
    
    Detected keywords: vomiting, diarrhea, limping, won't eat, lethargic, swelling, pain, blood in stool/urine
    
    **Action**: Schedule urgent appointment within 24 hours
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Routine">
    **Green Badge** - Regular appointment scheduling
    
    Detected keywords: wellness exam, vaccination, nail trim, general checkup
    
    **Action**: Schedule routine appointment at owner's convenience
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Info">
    **Blue Badge** - Informational call, no immediate medical need
    
    **Action**: Provide information, recommend resources, or schedule wellness visit
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Detected Information

Luna automatically extracts and displays:

- **Pet Name**: Detected from conversation ("My dog Bella...")
- **Species**: Dog, cat, bird, rabbit, etc.
- **Symptoms**: Live list of detected symptoms with red badges
- **Emergency Status**: Yes/No indicator

<Info>
  **How Detection Works**
  
  Luna analyzes every message in real-time, searching for medical keywords and extracting structured information. The triage panel updates automatically as new information is mentioned.
</Info>

## Natural Conversation Examples

### Example 1: Emergency Triage

**Caller**: "My dog Buddy ate chocolate about an hour ago. He's vomiting and seems really lethargic."

**Luna**: "I'm sorry to hear Buddy isn't feeling well. Chocolate ingestion can be serious. How much chocolate did he eat, and what's his weight?"

**Caller**: "He's about 30 pounds, and he ate maybe half a dark chocolate bar."

**Luna**: "Thank you. Based on what you've told me, Buddy needs to see a veterinarian right away. This is an emergency. Our clinic has an opening in 30 minutes at 2:00 PM with Dr. Chen. Would that work for you?"

**Triage Panel**:
- Pet: Buddy (dog)
- Symptoms: vomiting, lethargic, eaten chocolate, poisoned
- Urgency: 9/10 - EMERGENCY
- Appointment: Scheduled for 2:00 PM

### Example 2: Routine Appointment

**Caller**: "I'd like to schedule a wellness exam for my cat Luna."

**Luna**: "I'd be happy to help schedule Luna's wellness exam. How old is Luna, and when was her last visit?"

**Caller**: "She's 5 years old, and I think her last visit was about a year ago."

**Luna**: "Perfect, she's due for her annual checkup. We have availability this Thursday at 10:00 AM with Dr. Rivera, or Friday afternoon at 3:30 PM with Dr. Chen. Which works better for you?"

**Triage Panel**:
- Pet: Luna (cat)
- Species: cat
- Urgency: 1/10 - INFO/ROUTINE
- Appointment: Pending scheduling confirmation

### Example 3: Urgent Care

**Caller**: "My dog Max has been limping for two days and won't put weight on his right paw."

**Luna**: "I'm sorry Max is limping. Has there been any swelling, or did you notice when the limping started?"

**Caller**: "It started after we went to the dog park. His paw looks a little swollen."

**Luna**: "Thank you for that information. Max should be seen soon to check for sprains or injuries. I can get you in tomorrow morning at 9:00 AM with Dr. Chen, or this afternoon at 4:30 PM if it's more urgent."

**Triage Panel**:
- Pet: Max (dog)
- Symptoms: limping, swelling, pain, difficulty walking
- Urgency: 5/10 - URGENT
- Appointment: Offering same-day or next-day options

## Automatic Documentation

When a call ends, Luna automatically:

### 1. Saves Call Record

Creates a database entry including:
- **Caller information**: Name, phone, owner ID (if existing client)
- **Pet details**: Name, species
- **Call metadata**: Duration, timestamp, direction (inbound)
- **Triage data**: Urgency level, detected symptoms, keywords
- **Full transcript**: Complete conversation text
- **Summary**: AI-generated call summary

### 2. Creates Appointment (If Booked)

If an appointment was scheduled during the call:
- Auto-creates appointment record
- Links to pet and owner
- Sets triage level based on call analysis
- Includes symptoms and reason for visit
- Schedules for discussed date/time
- Sets status to "scheduled"

### 3. Logs to Audit Trail

Records the AI call event for compliance:
- User: "Luna AI"
- Action: "create"
- Resource: "call"
- Details: Caller, pet, and triage summary

<Tip>
  All saved data appears immediately in the [Appointments](/features/appointments) and [Medical Records](/features/medical-records) sections.
</Tip>

## Configuration

### Retell Agent ID

To use a custom Retell AI agent:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Agent ID">
    Log in to your Retell AI dashboard and copy your agent ID (format: `agent_xxxxxxxxxxxx`).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter in Settings">
    Click **Settings** in the Luna interface and paste your agent ID.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Call">
    Click **Start Call** to connect using your custom agent.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Accordion title="Environment Configuration">
  The default agent ID is set via the `VITE_RETELL_AGENT_ID` environment variable in your `.env` file:
  
  ```bash
  VITE_RETELL_AGENT_ID=agent_your_id_here
  ```
  
  Leave the Settings field empty to use the environment default.
</Accordion>

### Backend API

Luna requires a backend API endpoint at `/api/retell/create-web-call` that:

1. Receives the agent ID
2. Calls Retell AI to create a web call session
3. Returns the call ID and access token

Source code reference: `src/sections/VoiceSimulator.tsx:252`

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't Start Call - No Agent ID">
    **Error**: "No Retell Agent ID configured"
    
    **Solution**:
    - Set `VITE_RETELL_AGENT_ID` in your `.env` file, OR
    - Click Settings and enter your agent ID manually
    - Restart the application after changing `.env`
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Connection Stuck on 'Connecting'">
    **Possible Causes**:
    - Backend server not running
    - Invalid agent ID
    - Retell AI service issue
    - Network/firewall blocking WebRTC
    
    **Solutions**:
    - Verify backend is running (`npm run dev:server`)
    - Check browser console for error details
    - Test agent ID in Retell AI dashboard
    - Try different network (disable VPN)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Microphone Not Working">
    **Symptoms**: Luna can't hear you, or "No microphone access"
    
    **Solutions**:
    - Click the lock icon in browser address bar
    - Grant microphone permissions for the site
    - Check no other app is using the microphone
    - Test microphone in system settings
    - Refresh the page and try again
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Luna Can't Hear Me Clearly">
    **Symptoms**: Poor transcription, repeated "I didn't understand"
    
    **Solutions**:
    - Speak clearly and at normal volume
    - Reduce background noise (close doors, turn off fans)
    - Move closer to microphone (built-in or headset)
    - Use a headset for better audio quality
    - Check microphone isn't muted at hardware level
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Call Disconnects Unexpectedly">
    **Possible Causes**:
    - Unstable internet connection
    - Browser tab backgrounded/minimized
    - System sleep/standby
    
    **Solutions**:
    - Ensure stable internet connection
    - Keep browser tab active during call
    - Disable sleep mode during calls
    - Check browser console for error messages
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="No Triage Data Appearing">
    **Cause**: Symptoms not detected in conversation
    
    **Solution**:
    - Use explicit medical terms ("vomiting" vs "throwing up")
    - Mention species ("my dog" or "my cat")
    - State pet name clearly
    - Detection requires keywords from the predefined list (see source: `VoiceSimulator.tsx:45-56`)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy and Compliance

<Warning>
  **Call Recording Notice**
  
  All calls with Luna are recorded and transcribed for quality assurance and medical record keeping. Ensure compliance with local call recording laws.
</Warning>

### Data Security

- All calls are encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
- Transcripts stored securely in your database
- Audio recordings (if enabled) stored on Retell AI servers
- Access controlled by user authentication
- Audit trail maintained for all calls

### Compliance Considerations

- **HIPAA**: Configure Retell AI Business Associate Agreement
- **Two-Party Consent**: Ensure compliance with state/local recording laws
- **Data Retention**: Configure retention policies in Retell dashboard
- **PHI Protection**: Transcripts may contain protected health information

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="WebRTC Connection">
    When you click Start Call, the browser establishes a WebRTC connection to Retell AI servers using the access token from your backend.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Voice Streaming">
    Your microphone audio streams in real-time to Retell AI. Luna's voice streams back to your speakers.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Live Transcription">
    Retell AI transcribes the conversation in real-time and sends transcript updates via WebSocket.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Triage Analysis">
    Your frontend analyzes each transcript message for symptom keywords and updates the triage panel.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Auto-Save">
    When the call ends, a React effect hook saves the call record, transcript, and triage data to Supabase.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  **Technical Reference**
  
  Luna is built using the Retell Web Client SDK. Source code: `src/sections/VoiceSimulator.tsx`
  
  Key technologies:
  - Retell AI for conversational voice
  - WebRTC for real-time audio
  - React hooks for state management
  - Supabase for data persistence
</Info>

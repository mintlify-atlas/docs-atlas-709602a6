## Overview

The Appointment Scheduling system manages your clinic's calendar with built-in triage, multi-veterinarian support, and automated status tracking. Book wellness exams, urgent care, emergencies, surgeries, and follow-ups—all from a unified interface.

## Appointment Types

### Type Descriptions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Wellness">
    **Purpose**: Routine preventive care
    
    **Typical Duration**: 30 minutes
    
    **Includes**:
    - Physical examination
    - Vaccinations
    - Parasite prevention
    - Wellness bloodwork
    - Nutritional counseling
    
    **Triage**: Usually "routine" level
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Urgent">
    **Purpose**: Symptomatic care needing prompt attention
    
    **Typical Duration**: 30-45 minutes
    
    **Includes**:
    - Same-day or next-day booking
    - Problem-focused exam
    - Diagnostics
    - Treatment plan
    
    **Triage**: "Urgent" level
    
    **Examples**: Vomiting/diarrhea, limping, lethargy, decreased appetite
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Emergency">
    **Purpose**: Life-threatening conditions
    
    **Typical Duration**: 60+ minutes
    
    **Includes**:
    - Immediate triage
    - Stabilization
    - Emergency diagnostics
    - Critical care
    
    **Triage**: "Emergency" level
    
    **Examples**: Hit by car, seizures, difficulty breathing, poisoning, bloat
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Dental">
    **Purpose**: Oral health procedures
    
    **Typical Duration**: 1-3 hours
    
    **Includes**:
    - Dental prophylaxis (cleaning)
    - Dental radiographs
    - Extractions if needed
    - Oral surgery
    
    **Requires**: Pre-anesthetic bloodwork, anesthesia
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Surgery">
    **Purpose**: Surgical procedures
    
    **Typical Duration**: 1-4 hours depending on procedure
    
    **Includes**:
    - Pre-operative exam
    - Anesthesia
    - Surgical procedure
    - Recovery monitoring
    
    **Examples**: Spay/neuter, mass removal, orthopedic surgery, exploratory laparotomy
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Grooming">
    **Purpose**: Cosmetic and hygiene services
    
    **Typical Duration**: 30-60 minutes
    
    **Includes**:
    - Nail trims
    - Ear cleaning
    - Anal gland expression
    - Bathing (if offered)
    
    **Triage**: "Info" or "routine" level
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Follow-Up">
    **Purpose**: Post-treatment monitoring
    
    **Typical Duration**: 15-30 minutes
    
    **Includes**:
    - Recheck exam
    - Suture removal
    - Medication adjustment
    - Progress assessment
    
    **Links**: Reference to original appointment or medical record
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Status Workflow

Typical appointment lifecycle:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pending">
    Appointment created but not confirmed by owner. 
    
    **Actions**: Send confirmation request, phone follow-up
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirmed">
    Owner confirmed they will attend.
    
    **Actions**: Send 24-hour reminder, prepare patient file
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Checked In">
    Patient arrived at clinic, waiting for veterinarian.
    
    **Actions**: Update status when front desk checks in patient
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="In Progress">
    Veterinarian is currently examining the patient.
    
    **Actions**: Create SOAP notes, perform procedures
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Completed">
    Appointment finished, patient discharged.
    
    **Actions**: Finalize medical record, process payment, schedule follow-up if needed
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Alternative Outcomes

- **Cancelled**: Owner cancelled before appointment
- **No Show**: Owner didn't show up or call
- **Rescheduled**: Moved to different date/time

<Warning>
  **No-Show Tracking**
  
  Monitor no-show rates by filtering appointments with "No Show" status. High no-show rates may indicate need for better reminder systems.
</Warning>

## Best Practices

### Scheduling Guidelines

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Buffer Time">
    Allow buffer between appointments:
    - Wellness: 30-45 min slots
    - Urgent/sick: 45-60 min slots
    - Emergency: Flexible, may bump routine appointments
    - Surgery: Block 2-4 hours depending on procedure
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Triage Accuracy">
    Set appropriate triage levels:
    - **Emergency**: Life-threatening only (bleeding, seizures, difficulty breathing)
    - **Urgent**: Needs same-day or next-day (vomiting, limping, lethargy)
    - **Routine**: Can wait a few days (wellness, vaccinations)
    - **Info**: Consultative, non-medical (nail trim, questions)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Status Updates">
    Keep status current:
    - Update to "Confirmed" when owner confirms
    - Change to "Checked In" when patient arrives
    - Set "In Progress" when vet enters exam room
    - Mark "Completed" when patient leaves
    - This helps front desk and team coordination
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Documentation">
    Record important details:
    - Accurate reason for visit
    - All reported symptoms
    - Special instructions (aggressive pet, needs muzzle)
    - Owner requests (same vet, specific time)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't Find Appointment">
    **Try these steps**:
    1. Clear all filters (type, status, search)
    2. Search by pet name, owner name, or date
    3. Check if appointment was cancelled or rescheduled
    4. Verify date range (may be in past or far future)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Today's Schedule Not Showing">
    **Possible reasons**:
    - No appointments scheduled for today
    - All today's appointments are cancelled/completed
    - Date/time on system incorrect
    
    **Verify**: Check date filter and system clock
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Veterinarian Not Listed">
    **Cause**: Vet not in database
    
    **Solution**: Add veterinarian via admin panel or contact system administrator
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

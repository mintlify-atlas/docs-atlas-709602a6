## Platform Overview

Paw & Care is available on multiple platforms to fit your workflow:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iOS App">
    The iOS app is the primary interface for veterinarians and clinical staff, optimized for quick access to patient records, voice dictation, and appointment management.
    
    **Key Capabilities:**
    - Voice-to-SOAP note generation
    - Offline mode with automatic sync
    - Camera integration for patient photos
    - Push notifications for emergencies
    - Biometric authentication
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Web Application">
    The web app provides comprehensive practice management, advanced reporting, and administrative functions for office managers and administrators.
    
    **Key Capabilities:**
    - Advanced analytics and reporting
    - Billing and invoice management
    - Template customization
    - User and role management
    - Audit log access
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Technology Stack

Paw & Care is built on modern, reliable technologies:

- **Frontend**: React + TypeScript with shadcn/ui components
- **Mobile**: Capacitor for native iOS capabilities
- **Backend**: Supabase (PostgreSQL, authentication, storage)
- **AI Services**: OpenAI GPT-4, Whisper, and Retell AI
- **Real-time Sync**: WebSocket-based data synchronization

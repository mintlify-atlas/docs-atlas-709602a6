## Overview

Paw & Care's intelligent appointment system reduces no-shows by **40%** through automated reminders and streamlines scheduling with AI-powered booking. This guide covers the complete appointment lifecycle from initial booking through completion and follow-up.

## Appointment Status Workflow

### Status State Machine

Appointments progress through a defined workflow:

```
Pending → Scheduled → Confirmed → Checked In → In Progress → Completed
                     ↓                          ↓
                  Cancelled                  No Show
```

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Pending" icon="clock">
    **Initial state when created**
    
    - Just booked, awaiting confirmation
    - No reminders sent yet
    - Can be freely modified or cancelled
    - Owner expects confirmation communication
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Scheduled" icon="calendar-check">
    **Confirmed slot, awaiting appointment date**
    
    - Confirmation sent to owner
    - Appears on veterinarian's schedule
    - Automated reminders will be sent
    - Counted in daily appointment metrics
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Confirmed" icon="circle-check">
    **Owner confirmed they will attend**
    
    - Owner replied "CONFIRM" to reminder SMS
    - Or confirmed via phone/email
    - Lower risk of no-show
    - Priority for day-of scheduling
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Checked In" icon="door-open">
    **Patient arrived at clinic**
    
    - Owner and pet present in clinic
    - Waiting for examination
    - Veterinarian notified of arrival
    - Vitals may be recorded by technician
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="In Progress" icon="stethoscope">
    **Examination underway**
    
    - Veterinarian actively seeing patient
    - Documentation being created
    - Treatment in progress
    - Estimated completion time shown
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Completed" icon="check-circle">
    **Visit finished successfully**
    
    - Medical record finalized
    - Billing generated
    - Follow-up scheduled if needed
    - Archived for reporting
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Cancelled" icon="x-circle">
    **Appointment cancelled by owner or clinic**
    
    - Time slot freed for re-booking
    - Reason documented
    - Can reschedule if desired
    - Tracked for analytics
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="No Show" icon="user-x">
    **Owner did not arrive**
    
    - Auto-marked if not checked in within 15 minutes of scheduled time
    - Impacts no-show rate metric
    - May trigger follow-up call or email
    - Flag for future scheduling
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Booking Appointments

### Method 1: Manual Booking (Front Desk)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Appointment Scheduler">
    Navigate to **Appointments** tab → Tap **+ New Appointment** button
    
    <Tip>
      Keyboard shortcut on desktop: `Cmd+N` or `Ctrl+N` to quickly create new appointment
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill Appointment Details">
    Complete the appointment form:
    
    **Required Fields**:
    - **Pet Name**: Select from existing patients or enter new
    - **Owner Name**: Auto-populated if pet exists, otherwise enter
    - **Date**: Select from calendar picker
    - **Time**: Choose from available slots (15/30/45/60 minute increments)
    - **Type**: Select appointment type
    - **Veterinarian**: Assign to specific vet based on availability
    
    **Appointment Types**:
    - **Wellness**: Annual exam, preventive care
    - **Urgent**: Same-day concerns, not life-threatening
    - **Emergency**: Immediate life-threatening conditions
    - **Dental**: Teeth cleaning, oral surgery
    - **Surgery**: Spay/neuter, soft tissue, orthopedic
    - **Grooming**: Nail trim, bathing, hygiene
    - **Follow-Up**: Recheck, suture removal, progress check
    
    **Optional Fields**:
    - **Reason/Symptoms**: Free text description
    - **Duration**: Override default slot length
    - **Notes**: Internal staff notes
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Availability">
    System automatically checks for conflicts:
    
    **Available Slot** (Green):
    - Time is open for selected veterinarian
    - No overlapping appointments
    - Within clinic hours (default 8 AM - 6 PM)
    
    **Conflict Warning** (Yellow):
    - Veterinarian has another appointment nearby
    - May cause scheduling tight squeeze
    - Suggested alternatives displayed
    
    **Double-Booking** (Red):
    - Same time slot already filled
    - Only allowed for emergencies with override
    - Warning modal: "This will double-book Dr. Chen at 2:00 PM. Continue?"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm and Save">
    Tap **Schedule Appointment** button
    
    **Automatic Actions**:
    1. Appointment created with status: **Scheduled**
    2. Confirmation number generated (e.g., APT-20260305-001)
    3. Confirmation email sent to owner immediately
    4. Appointment added to veterinarian's calendar
    5. Reminder jobs scheduled:
       - 24-hour reminder: Sent at same time day before
       - 2-hour reminder: Sent 2 hours before appointment
    
    <Note>
      Confirmation email template uses dynamic variables: {{petName}}, {{ownerName}}, {{date}}, {{time}}, {{vetName}}, {{clinicPhone}}
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Method 2: AI-Powered Booking (Phone)

Luna (AI receptionist) can book appointments autonomously during calls:

<Steps>
  <Step title="AI Greeting and Intent Detection">
    When owner calls clinic:
    
    ```
    AI: "Thank you for calling Paw & Care Veterinary Clinic. 
         This is Luna, how can I help you today?"
    
    Owner: "I'd like to schedule a checkup for my dog Bella."
    
    AI: "I'd be happy to help you schedule a checkup for Bella. 
         May I have your name please?"
    
    Owner: "Sarah Miller."
    ```
    
    AI detects **booking intent** and begins appointment workflow.
  </Step>

  <Step title="AI Collects Information">
    Luna asks targeted questions:
    
    ```
    AI: "Great, Sarah. What's the reason for Bella's visit today?"
    
    Owner: "Just her annual wellness exam."
    
    AI: "Perfect. Is Bella experiencing any current health concerns 
         or is she feeling well?"
    
    Owner: "She's doing fine, just due for her annual."
    
    [AI assigns Triage Level: Routine]
    [AI logs symptoms: none, annual wellness]
    ```
    
    AI automatically:
    - Determines triage level based on symptoms
    - Extracts pet species from context or asks
    - Notes appointment type (wellness)
  </Step>

  <Step title="AI Checks Availability">
    Luna queries scheduling database in real-time:
    
    ```
    AI: "Let me check our availability. What day works best 
         for you?"
    
    Owner: "Sometime next week, maybe Tuesday or Wednesday?"
    
    AI: "I have several openings. On Tuesday I have 10:00 AM, 
         1:30 PM, or 4:00 PM available. On Wednesday I have 
         9:00 AM, 11:30 AM, or 3:00 PM. Which time would you prefer?"
    
    Owner: "Tuesday at 10 AM works great."
    ```
    
    **Availability Logic**:
    - Queries veterinarian schedules from database
    - Respects blocked time slots and lunch breaks
    - Suggests times based on appointment type duration
    - Avoids double-booking unless emergency
  </Step>

  <Step title="AI Books Appointment">
    ```
    AI: "Perfect. I've scheduled Bella's annual wellness exam 
         for Tuesday, March 12th at 10:00 AM with Dr. Emily Chen. 
         You should receive a confirmation email shortly at 
         the address we have on file. Is there anything else 
         I can help you with today?"
    
    Owner: "No, that's all. Thank you!"
    
    AI: "You're welcome! We'll see Bella on Tuesday at 10:00 AM. 
         Have a great day!"
    ```
    
    **Database Actions**:
    1. Appointment record created with:
       - Pet: Bella (linked to existing patient ID if found)
       - Owner: Sarah Miller (linked or new record)
       - Date: March 12, 2026
       - Time: 10:00 AM
       - Type: Wellness
       - Triage: Routine
       - Vet: Dr. Emily Chen (auto-assigned based on availability)
       - Status: Scheduled
       - Call ID: Linked to source call for audit trail
    
    2. Automated reminders scheduled
    3. Confirmation email sent
    4. Call log saved with full transcript
  </Step>
</Steps>

**AI Booking Success Rate**: 80%+ of routine appointments booked without human intervention

## Automated Reminder System

### Reminder Schedule

<Steps>
  <Step title="24-Hour Email Reminder">
    **Sent**: Exactly 24 hours before appointment (same time)
    
    **Example**: Appointment Tuesday at 2:00 PM → Reminder sent Monday at 2:00 PM
    
    **Content**:
    ```
    Subject: Appointment Reminder - Bella Tomorrow at 2:00 PM
    
    Hi Sarah,
    
    This is a friendly reminder that Bella has an appointment 
    tomorrow (Tuesday, March 12th) at 2:00 PM with Dr. Emily Chen 
    at Paw & Care Veterinary Clinic.
    
    Appointment Details:
    - Patient: Bella
    - Type: Annual Wellness Exam
    - Date: Tuesday, March 12, 2026
    - Time: 2:00 PM
    - Veterinarian: Dr. Emily Chen
    - Location: 123 Main Street, Cityville, CA
    
    Please arrive 10 minutes early to complete any necessary 
    paperwork. If you need to cancel or reschedule, please call 
    us at (555) 123-4567.
    
    We look forward to seeing you and Bella!
    
    Best regards,
    Paw & Care Veterinary Clinic
    ```
    
    **Delivery Tracking**:
    - Email sent via SendGrid API
    - Open tracking enabled
    - Click tracking for phone/address links
    - Bounce/error handling with retry logic
  </Step>

  <Step title="24-Hour SMS Reminder">
    **Sent**: Same time as email (24 hours before)
    
    **Content**:
    ```
    Reminder: Bella has appointment tomorrow 3/12 at 2:00 PM 
    with Dr. Chen at Paw & Care. Reply CONFIRM to confirm or 
    call (555) 123-4567 to reschedule.
    ```
    
    **Interactive Responses**:
    - Reply "CONFIRM" → Appointment status updated to "Confirmed"
    - Reply "CANCEL" → Triggers cancellation workflow
    - Reply "RESCHEDULE" → AI-assisted rebooking initiated
    - Reply "STOP" → Owner opted out of SMS (email only)
    
    **Delivery via Twilio**:
    - SMS sent to owner's registered phone number
    - Delivery status tracked (sent, delivered, failed)
    - Failed SMS logged and flagged for manual follow-up
  </Step>

  <Step title="2-Hour Push Notification">
    **Sent**: 2 hours before appointment (iOS app users only)
    
    **Notification**:
    ```
    📅 Bella's appointment in 2 hours
    
    2:00 PM today with Dr. Emily Chen
    
    Tap to view details or check in
    ```
    
    **Actions**:
    - Tap notification → Opens appointment details in app
    - Quick action: "Check In Now" (if within 30 minutes)
    - Snooze for 30 minutes
    - Dismiss (no action)
    
    **Delivery via Apple Push Notification Service (APNS)**:
    - Sent to devices with app installed
    - Haptic feedback (vibration)
    - Badge count increments on app icon
    - Falls back to SMS if push fails
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email Settings">

    - Custom clinic logo and branding
    - Modify email template text
    - Add/remove dynamic variables
    - Set sender name and reply-to address
    - Include custom instructions (parking, forms, etc.)

    - `{{petName}}` - Patient name
    - `{{ownerName}}` - Owner first and last name
    - `{{date}}` - Formatted appointment date
    - `{{time}}` - Appointment time
    - `{{vetName}}` - Veterinarian full name
    - `{{appointmentType}}` - Type of visit
    - `{{clinicName}}` - Practice name
    - `{{clinicPhone}}` - Clinic phone number
    - `{{clinicAddress}}` - Full address
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="SMS Settings">
    **Configuration Options**:
    - Enable/disable SMS reminders
    - Customize message text (160 character limit)
    - Configure interactive keywords (CONFIRM, CANCEL)
    - Set SMS sender name/number
    - Opt-out handling
    
    **Best Practices**:
    - Keep under 160 characters to avoid splitting
    - Include clear call-to-action
    - Provide opt-out instructions
    - Test messages before enabling
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Timing Settings">
    **Reminder Schedule**:
    - 24-hour reminder: Enable/disable, adjust timing
    - 2-hour reminder: Enable/disable, iOS only option
    - Custom reminder: Add third reminder at custom interval
    
    **Business Rules**:
    - Only send reminders during daytime hours (8 AM - 8 PM)
    - Skip reminders for same-day appointments less than 24h away
    - Emergency appointments get immediate confirmation only
    - Confirmed appointments still receive 2-hour reminder
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  **40% No-Show Reduction**: Automated reminders with two touchpoints (24h and 2h) reduce no-shows by 40% compared to no reminder system.

## Day-of Appointment Flow

### Morning: Veterinarian Preparation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Today's Schedule">
    Navigate to **Dashboard** → **Today's Schedule** card
    
    **Schedule View**:
    - All appointments for current day
    - Color-coded by status:
      - Gray: Scheduled (not yet confirmed)
      - Green: Confirmed (owner replied to reminder)
      - Blue: Checked In (patient arrived)
      - Purple: In Progress (examination underway)
      - Check: Completed
    - Sorted chronologically by time slot
    - Triage level badges (Emergency/Urgent/Routine)
    - Estimated duration shown
    
    <Tip>
      Desktop users can print daily schedule: Dashboard → Today's Schedule → Print button
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pre-Appointment Patient Review">
    Tap appointment card to view details:
    
    **Quick View Modal**:
    - Pet name, species, breed, age, weight
    - Owner name and contact information
    - Appointment type and reason
    - Triage level and symptoms
    - Time and duration
    
    **Action Buttons**:
    - **View Patient Record**: Opens full medical history
    - **View Previous SOAP Notes**: Last 3 visits
    - **Call Owner**: One-tap dialing
    - **Edit Appointment**: Modify time or details
    - **Start Examination**: Changes status to "In Progress"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Patient Record Review">
    Tap **View Patient Record** to access:
    
    **Critical Information**:
    - **Allergies**: Drug sensitivities, food allergies
    - **Current Medications**: Name, dose, frequency
    - **Known Conditions**: Chronic diseases, past diagnoses
    - **Vaccination Status**: Due dates highlighted in red if overdue
    - **Previous Visits**: Last exam findings, trends
    - **Weight History**: Graph showing weight changes
    
    <Note>
      All data syncs in real-time across devices. Updates made on desktop immediately visible on mobile and vice versa (within 3 seconds).
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Reception: Check-In Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Owner Arrival">
    When owner arrives at clinic:
    
    **Front Desk Actions**:
    1. Greet owner and confirm pet name
    2. Open **Appointments** tab on iPad/desktop
    3. Find appointment (usually at top of list for current time)
    4. Tap appointment card
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update Status to Checked In">
    In appointment detail modal:
    
    1. Tap **Status** dropdown
    2. Select **Checked In**
    3. System automatically:
       - Timestamps check-in time
       - Sends push notification to assigned veterinarian: "Bella has arrived for 2:00 PM appointment"
       - Moves appointment to "Active" section of schedule
       - Starts wait time clock
    
    **Additional Check-In Actions**:
    - Update contact information if changed
    - Collect payment method for new clients
    - Have owner sign consent forms (digital or paper)
    - Technician can pre-record vitals while waiting
  </Step>

  <Step title="Exam Room Assignment">
    **Optional**: Assign exam room number
    
    - Tap **Assign Room** button
    - Select room number (1-6)
    - Room indicator appears on appointment card
    - Veterinarian sees room assignment in notification
    
    Direct owner to waiting area or directly to exam room based on wait time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### During Examination

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start Examination">
    Veterinarian taps appointment card → **Start Examination** button
    
    **Automatic Actions**:
    - Status updates to **In Progress**
    - Start time recorded (for duration tracking)
    - Exam room indicator turns purple (occupied)
    - Front desk sees appointment is active
  </Step>

  <Step title="Document Visit">
    Two workflows:
    
    **A. Real-Time Dictation** (Preferred):
    - Open **Dictate** tab
    - Patient auto-selected (current appointment)
    - Record voice notes during examination
    - Generate SOAP notes immediately after
    - Save record as draft or finalized
    
    **B. Post-Exam Documentation**:
    - Complete examination
    - Mark appointment "Completed"
    - Document later from **Medical Records** tab
    - Link record to appointment ID
    
    <Tip>
      Real-time dictation reduces documentation time by 70% and ensures no details are forgotten.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update Appointment Notes">
    Quick notes can be added directly to appointment:
    
    - Tap appointment card
    - Scroll to **Notes** section
    - Add text notes visible to all staff:
      - Diagnosis summary
      - Treatment provided
      - Client instructions given
      - Follow-up needed
    
    Notes sync immediately across all devices.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Completing Appointment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Finalize Medical Record">
    Before marking appointment complete:
    
    1. **Generate SOAP Notes**: Dictation → AI generation → Review
    2. **Review Clinical Insights**: Accept/reject AI suggestions
    3. **Save Medical Record**: Status → Finalized
    4. **Link to Appointment**: Automatic if created from appointment context
    
    Record ID appears on appointment card after saving.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Billing">
    From SOAP note or appointment:
    
    1. Tap **Generate Billing** button
    2. AI extracts billable items from notes:
       - Office visit/examination fees
       - Procedures performed
       - Diagnostics ordered
       - Medications dispensed
       - Supplies used
    3. Review and adjust line items
    4. Save billing record
    5. Send invoice to front desk
    
    **Billing statuses**:
    - Draft: Being created
    - Finalized: Ready for payment
    - Paid: Payment received
  </Step>

  <Step title="Schedule Follow-Up (If Needed)">
    If recheck or follow-up required:
    
    1. From appointment detail, tap **Schedule Follow-Up**
    2. System suggests date based on diagnosis:
       - "Recheck in 2 weeks" → Suggests date 14 days out
       - "Suture removal in 10 days" → Suggests date 10 days out
    3. Pre-fills appointment with:
       - Same patient and owner
       - Type: Follow-Up
       - Linked to original appointment
       - Notes reference original visit
    4. Select time and veterinarian
    5. Save appointment
  </Step>

  <Step title="Mark Appointment Completed">
    After all tasks finished:
    
    1. Tap appointment card
    2. Change status to **Completed**
    3. System records:
       - Actual end time (for duration analytics)
       - Total visit duration
       - Linked medical record ID
       - Linked billing record ID
       - Follow-up appointment ID (if scheduled)
    
    Appointment moves to completed queue and frees exam room.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Handling No-Shows

### Automatic No-Show Detection

<Steps>
  <Step title="15-Minute Grace Period">
    If appointment not marked "Checked In" by 15 minutes after scheduled time:
    
    **System Actions**:
    1. Status automatically updates to **No Show** (orange badge)
    2. Front desk receives notification: "Bella's 2:00 PM appointment not checked in"
    3. Time slot remains blocked for 15 more minutes (30 minutes total)
    4. After 30 minutes, slot becomes available for walk-ins
  </Step>

  <Step title="Manual Override">
    If owner calls or arrives late:
    
    **Front Desk Actions**:
    1. Find appointment (filter by No Show status)
    2. Change status back to **Checked In**
    3. Notify veterinarian of late arrival
    4. Adjust schedule if needed (may need to wait for next opening)
    
    **Or Reschedule**:
    1. Change status to **Cancelled** with reason "No show - rescheduled"
    2. Create new appointment for different time
    3. Send confirmation for new appointment
  </Step>

  <Step title="Follow-Up Workflow">
    **Recommended no-show protocol**:
    
    1. **Day of**: Call owner 1-2 hours after missed appointment
       - "We missed you today for Bella's 2 PM appointment. Is everything okay?"
       - Offer to reschedule
       - Note reason for no-show
    
    2. **Next Day**: Send email if no response to call
       - Use **Email Templates** → "No-Show Follow-Up"
       - Include easy reschedule link or phone number
    
    3. **Track Pattern**: Review no-show history
       - Navigate to **Appointments** → Filter: **No Show** → Group by owner
       - Clients with 3+ no-shows flagged for policy discussion
       - Consider requiring advance payment or deposit
  </Step>
</Steps>

### No-Show Analytics

Track no-show performance:

**Dashboard Metric**: **No-Show Rate**

**Calculation**: (No-Shows / Total Appointments) × 100

**Target**: Less than 10%

**View Details**: Dashboard → Click "No-Show Rate" metric → See breakdown by:
- Day of week (Mondays typically higher)
- Time of day (late afternoon appointments more risky)
- Appointment type (routine vs. urgent)
- Client demographics (new clients vs. established)

## Cancellation and Rescheduling

### Owner-Initiated Cancellation

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Phone Cancellation">
    **AI Handles Routine Cancellations**:
    
    ```
    Owner: "I need to cancel Bella's appointment on Tuesday."
    
    AI: "I can help with that. Let me pull up Bella's appointment. 
         I see she has an appointment on Tuesday, March 12th at 
         10:00 AM. Is that the one you'd like to cancel?"
    
    Owner: "Yes, that's the one."
    
    AI: "No problem. I've cancelled that appointment. Would you 
         like to reschedule for a different time?"
    
    Owner: "Not right now, I'll call back later."
    
    AI: "Understood. The appointment has been cancelled. Feel free 
         to call anytime to reschedule. Is there anything else?"
    ```
    
    **System Actions**:
    - Appointment status → **Cancelled**
    - Cancellation reason: "Owner request"
    - Time slot freed for re-booking
    - Cancellation email sent to owner (confirmation)
    - Veterinarian schedule updated
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="SMS Cancellation">
    **Interactive SMS Response**:
    
    When owner replies "CANCEL" to reminder SMS:
    
    ```
    Reminder: Bella has appointment tomorrow 3/12 at 10:00 AM
    
    Owner replies: CANCEL
    
    Auto-response: Appointment cancelled. Reply RESCHEDULE for 
    new time or call (555) 123-4567. Reply STOP to opt out.
    ```
    
    **System Actions**:
    - Status → **Cancelled** automatically
    - Confirmation SMS sent
    - Slot freed immediately
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Manual Cancellation">
    **Staff-Initiated**:
    
    1. Find appointment in **Appointments** tab
    2. Tap appointment card → **Cancel Appointment** button
    3. Enter cancellation reason:
       - Owner request
       - Weather/emergency
       - Clinic closure
       - Veterinarian unavailable
       - Other (free text)
    4. Optionally offer to reschedule now
    5. Confirm cancellation
    6. System sends cancellation email to owner
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Rescheduling Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Existing Appointment">
    Navigate to **Appointments** → Search by owner or pet name
    
    Or access from:
    - Call log (if cancellation via phone)
    - Email notification (if owner clicked reschedule link)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Change Date/Time">
    Tap appointment card → **Reschedule** button
    
    **Reschedule Modal**:
    - Current appointment details shown at top
    - New date picker calendar
    - Available time slots filtered by:
      - Same veterinarian (preferred)
      - Same appointment type duration
      - Any veterinarian if original not available
    
    Select new date and time.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Reschedule">
    Tap **Confirm Reschedule** button
    
    **System Actions**:
    1. Old appointment status → **Cancelled** (reason: "Rescheduled")
    2. New appointment created with:
       - Same patient, owner, type, symptoms
       - New date and time
       - Status: **Scheduled**
       - Linked to original appointment (audit trail)
    3. Confirmation email sent with new appointment details
    4. New reminders scheduled (24h, 2h)
    5. Old reminders cancelled
    
    <Note>
      Original appointment preserved in database for analytics (counted as cancellation, not no-show).
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

    - Physical examination

    - Nutritional counseling

    **Default Duration**: 30 minutes
    
    **Typical Tasks**:
    - Symptom assessment
    - Diagnostics (blood work, X-rays)
    - Treatment (fluids, medication)
    - Monitoring
    - Discharge instructions
    
    **Template**: Standard SOAP
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Emergency" icon="siren">

    **Typical Tasks**:
    - Stabilization

    - Intensive monitoring
    - Owner communication

    - Pre-anesthetic exam and blood work
    - Anesthesia induction and monitoring
    - Full dental radiographs
    - Ultrasonic scaling and polishing
    - Extractions if needed
    - Recovery monitoring

## Advanced Scheduling Features

### Multi-Veterinarian Management

<Steps>
  <Step title="View All Veterinarian Schedules">
    Navigate to **Appointments** → **Calendar View**
    
    **Options**:
    - **Day View**: Hourly grid, all vets side-by-side
    - **Week View**: 7-day overview, color-coded by vet
    - **Month View**: Density heatmap showing busy days
    
    **Vet Filters**:
    - Show all veterinarians (default)
    - Filter to specific vet
    - Show only available vets for selected time
  </Step>

  <Step title="Block Time Slots">
    Practice Managers can block unavailable times:
    
    1. Navigate to **Appointments** → Select veterinarian
    2. Right-click time slot (or long-press on mobile)
    3. Select **Block Time**
    4. Enter reason:
       - Lunch break
       - Surgery (blocking multiple hours)
       - Continuing education
       - Vacation
       - Other
    5. Set duration or end time
    6. Confirm block
    
    Blocked slots show gray with "Unavailable" label and cannot be booked (except by override).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Load Balancing">
    System suggests veterinarian based on:
    
    **Factors**:
    - Current appointment load (avoid overloading one vet)
    - Specialization match (dental → dentist-trained vet)
    - Patient history (assign same vet for continuity)
    - Triage level (emergencies → most experienced)
    - Availability and schedule gaps
    
    **Override**: Manually assign different veterinarian if needed
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Bulk Operations

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Batch Reminders">
    **Send reminders to multiple appointments**:
    
    1. Navigate to **Appointments** → Filter: **Tomorrow**
    2. Select multiple appointments (checkbox)
    3. Tap **Send Reminders** button
    4. Choose channels: Email, SMS, or Both
    5. Preview message template
    6. Confirm send
    
    **Use Case**: Manually trigger reminders if automated system failed or for special clinic-wide announcements.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Reschedule Multiple">
    **Reschedule many appointments at once**:
    
    1. Filter appointments by date range or veterinarian
    2. Select multiple appointments
    3. Tap **Bulk Reschedule**
    4. Enter offset:
       - "Postpone all by 1 day"
       - "Move all 2 hours later"
    5. System checks for conflicts
    6. Review proposed new times
    7. Confirm bulk reschedule
    8. Confirmation emails sent automatically
    
    **Use Case**: Veterinarian out sick, clinic closure due to weather, equipment maintenance.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Cancel Multiple">
    **Cancel several appointments simultaneously**:
    
    1. Select multiple appointments
    2. Tap **Bulk Cancel**
    3. Enter cancellation reason (applies to all)
    4. Confirm cancellations
    5. Cancellation emails sent to all owners
    6. Option to include rescheduling instructions
    
    **Use Case**: Emergency clinic closure, natural disaster, power outage.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Reminders Not Sending">
    **Check**:
    - Email/SMS provider configuration in Settings
    - Owner has valid email/phone in profile
    - Reminders enabled for appointment type
    - Appointment status is "Scheduled" (not pending)
    
    **Manual Send**:
    - Tap appointment → **Send Reminder Now** button
    - Choose channel (email or SMS)
    - Confirm send
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Double-Booking Blocked">
    **If legitimate emergency**:
    - System warns but allows override
    - Click **"Override and Double-Book"** button
    - Enter justification reason
    - Veterinarian receives notification about double-booking
    
    **Avoid**:
    - Review schedule before booking
    - Use emergency slots (leave some unscheduled time)
    - Reschedule non-urgent appointments to accommodate emergency
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Appointment Not Syncing">
    **Symptoms**:
    - Created on mobile but not showing on desktop
    - Status update not reflecting across devices
    
    **Solutions**:
    - Pull-to-refresh on mobile (swipe down)
    - Check network connection (must be online)
    - Wait 5 seconds for sync to complete
    - If offline, changes queue and sync when connection restored
    - Force quit and reopen app if sync fails
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
